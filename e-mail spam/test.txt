Spam-like emails:

“Congratulations! You have won a $1,000 gift card. Click here to claim now.”

“Urgent: Your account has been compromised. Verify your details immediately.”

“Earn $5000 per week working from home. Sign up today!”

“Limited time offer! Buy one, get one free. Don’t miss out!”

“You’ve been selected for a free vacation to the Bahamas. Claim your ticket now.”

Non-spam (legitimate) emails:

“Hi John, can we reschedule our meeting to next Tuesday?”

“Please find attached the quarterly sales report for your review.”

“Thank you for your payment. Your invoice is attached for reference.”

“Reminder: The team meeting is at 3 PM in Conference Room A.”

“Can you provide feedback on the draft proposal by Friday?”
